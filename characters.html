Ben 10 Characters

Ben Tennyson

Ben Tennyson is the main character of the series. He wields the Omnitrix, which allows him to transform into various alien forms with unique abilities.

Gwen Tennyson

Gwen Tennyson is Ben's cousin and has magical abilities. She often assists Ben during their adventures.

Max Tennyson

Maxwell "Max" Tennyson is a retired Plumber and the paternal grandfather of Ben and Gwen.

Kevin Levin

Kevin Ethan Levin is an Osmosian, a mutant subspecies of human, who first appeared as one of Ben's enemies but reformed as his best friend and Gwen's love interest.

Rook Blonko

Rook Blonko is an alien from the fictional planet Revonnah. He serves as Ben's new partner throughout the series ever since after Gwen and Kevin left the team in Ommiverse.

Ben's Alien Forms

Heatblast

Heatblast is an alien form with the ability to generate and project intense heat and fire. He can manipulate flames for offensive and defensive purposes.

Special Powers:

  • Pyrokinesis (control over fire)
  • Heat and fire projection
  • Heat-resistant body

Four Arms

Four Arms is a powerful alien with incredible strength and four strong arms. He excels in hand-to-hand combat and can lift heavy objects with ease.

Special Powers:

  • Superhuman strength
  • Enhanced durability
  • Skilled hand-to-hand combatant
